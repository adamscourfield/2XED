# AX Currency Decision (v2)

## Decision
Primary spendable currency: **AX** (Anaxi Credits).

## Why AX
- Short, modern, and brandable.
- Works visually in compact HUD (`AX 245`).
- Feels secondary-appropriate (not childish like coins/gems).

## Player-facing naming
- Display label: **AX**
- Helper copy: "Earn AX for progress, persistence, and mastery."

## Principles
1. AX rewards effort + progress, not just perfection.
2. Bigger AX gains for mastery transitions and recovery.
3. AX is cosmetic/status utility, not academic grade.

## UX rule
AX balance should be visible at all times during learning and diagnostic flows.

## Do/Don’t
- Do: micro-reward pulses per answer.
- Do: stronger milestone animation for route complete / secure transition.
- Don’t: loud confetti every answer.
- Don’t: public wealth ranking by default.
